Welcome to Oasis Schools Arua

Nurturing Future Leaders with Excellence and Integrity

Oasis Schools Arua is a premier educational institution in the West Nile region of Uganda, providing quality education from Nursery through Primary levels. Established in 2014, we have grown to become a center of academic excellence known for our holistic approach to education that combines rigorous academics with strong moral values.

Our campuses in Arua and Koboko offer state-of-the-art facilities, experienced educators, and a nurturing environment where every child can thrive. We believe in educating the whole child - mind, body, and spirit - to prepare them for the challenges of tomorrow.

At Oasis Schools, we don't just teach students; we inspire learners, nurture talents, and build character. Our graduates leave us as confident, compassionate individuals ready to make a positive impact in their communities.

Our Core Identity

The foundation of everything we do at Oasis Schools

Our Mission

To provide quality, holistic, and accessible education that nurtures young minds into future leaders with integrity, innovation, and a passion for lifelong learning. We are committed to creating an environment that fosters academic excellence, character development, and social responsibility.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in the West Nile region, recognized for producing well-rounded graduates who excel academically, demonstrate strong moral character, and contribute meaningfully to society. We aspire to set the standard for 21st-century education in Uganda.
